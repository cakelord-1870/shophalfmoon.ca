    Natural Cork Massage Roller

    Our Natural Cork Massage Roller allows you to roll out tight muscles while helping to improve circulation. Made from natural cork, this firm roller is a powerful self-healing tool that can improve mobility and recovery, and calm your nervous system. Lean into a wall, or the floor to create just the right pressure to roll out your back or big muscle groups like hips and legs. Roll along arms to relieve wrist and hand pain.


    FEATURES

    • Small and lightweight so you can take it with you to yoga or the gym

    • Provides firm pressure

    • Releases tight muscles

    • Narrow and fairly localized pressure

    • Improves circulation and mobility

    • Use on large muscle groups or under feet/arms/wrists


    SPECS

    • 11.5” x 2.1” x 2.1”

    • 100% Natural Cork

    • Made in Taiwan


    PRODUCT CARE

    • Clean the cork when/if needed

    • Use a wet cloth and allow to air dry

    • Do not soak in water and avoid direct sunlight
