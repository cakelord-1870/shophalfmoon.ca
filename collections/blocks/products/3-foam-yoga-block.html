    3" Foam Yoga Block

    $16.00

    Color

    Our 3" Foam Yoga Block gives you a mild, medium, or intense lift depending on which end you prop it on. This allows you to have comfort, stability and grounding throughout your asana practice. These foam blocks are lightweight for easy mobility while you practice and are gentle on the body.


    FEATURES

    • Soft & comfortable

    • Moderate density

    • Lightweight


    SPECS

    • 9" x 6" x 3" (23 cm x 15 cm x 7.6 cm)

    • 4 oz (113 g)

    • 100% EVA foam spray

    • Made in Taiwan


    PRODUCT CARE

    • Spray with our Mat Cleanser, or a damp cloth with a mild/natural disinfectant (vinegar, mild soap)

    • Wipe and air dry before storage
