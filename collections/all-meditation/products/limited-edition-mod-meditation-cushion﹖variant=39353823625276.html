    Limited Edition Mod Meditation Cushion

    $90.00

    Color
    Fresco
    Solstice

    Out Of Stock!

    We will notify you when this product becomes available.

    Our Mod Meditation Cushion is designed to help you meditate comfortably for longer by providing enough height to allow your hips to soften and relax. This modern shape is both beautiful and functional in the studio or at home. This Mod Meditation Cushion is the highest of our meditation cushion line, making it the go-to pick for those with very tight hips.

    TIP: For the best sitting posture, make sure your knees are lower than your hips.

    This product is part of our Halfmoon Limited Edition Collection is a limited time offering showcasing Halfmoon's creative vision for that season. Carefully selecting patterns and prints allows us to bring to life modern, quality and current fabrics that will enhance and elevate your studio, home or zen space. Reinvigorate life into your meditation and yoga practice with these curated selections.


    FEATURES

    • Adjustable support for optimal sitting posture

    • Malleable buckwheat fill shapes to any seat

    • Handy carry handle

    • 5.5" of height/lift is great to relax tighter hips


    SPECS

    • 13" x 5.5" (33 cm x 14 cm)

    • 6.0 lbs (2.7 kg)

    • Cushions are filled by volume. Due to the natural differences in buckwheat, weight may vary by 5%

    • Cover: 100% cotton

    • Filling: 100% natural Canadian buckwheat hulls

    • Handcrafted just outside Vancouver, BC, Canada


    PRODUCT CARE

    • Empty the buckwheat filling into a large container

    • Wash the cover in a cold water washer setting, and hang to dry

    • Use a funnel to insert the buckwheat hulls back into the cover (you can even make a funnel but rolling up a piece of paper and inserting it into the opening)

    • Do not bleach


    NOTES

    • There is no inner casing for the whole buckwheat hulls. Unzip your cushion carefully to empty the hulls for cover cleaning.
