    Mighty Mat

    $86.00

    Color
    Charcoal

    Out Of Stock!

    We will notify you when this product becomes available.

    Our Mighty Mat is back! By popular demand this sticky, durable and comfortable mat has been added back to the Halfmoon Mat lineup. A great mat for any style of yoga, the 4mm thick Mighty Mat is a great mat for your self-practice or your studio practice.

    TIP: Clean your mat thoroughly before use to enhance traction and ease of use. 


    FEATURES

    • Durable and comfortable

    • Sticky for traction and stability

    • Traction improves with washing and use

    • Easy to clean

    • Great for any style yoga

    • Performs well even with sweat

    • Pairs with our Wet Grip Towel or Gripster Towel for enhanced grip! 


    SPECS

    • 72" x 24" (183cm x 61cm)

    • 4 mm thick 

    • Made of non-toxic PVC (6P Free)

    • Made in Taiwan


    PRODUCT CARE

    • Due to a finish on the mat used to avoid it sticking to itself while packaged, we recommend you clean the mat before using for optimal performance. Repeat as needed

    • Spray with water or our Mat Cleanser (or gentle cleaning product) and wipe with a sponge or cloth

    • Hang or lay flat to air dry
