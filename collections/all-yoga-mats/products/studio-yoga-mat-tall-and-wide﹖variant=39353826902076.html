    Studio Yoga Mat Tall and Wide

    $56.00

    Color
    Charcoal

    Out Of Stock!

    We will notify you when this product becomes available.

    Our Tall + Wide Essential studio mat is a taller, wider version of our most widely-used mat, the Essential Studio Mat. At 84” x 27”, this mat is ideal for tall yogis or any yogi who likes a little extra space while they practice. Ideal for any yoga practice from energetic to restorative, at the studio, or at home.


    FEATURES

    • Latex-free

    • Extra-long & wide

    • Grippy & durable

    • Non-toxic PVC (6p free)


    SPECS

    • 4mm (3/16 inch) thick

    • 84” x 27”

    • 3.2 lbs

    • Non-toxic PVC (6p free)

    • Made in Taiwan


    PRODUCT CARE

    • Before using your mat for the first time, clean it thoroughly, then repeat as needed 

    • Spray with water or our Mat Cleanser, then wipe clean with a dry cloth

    • Air dry before storage
